Congress protest throws traffic out of gear in Bengaluru

The traffic police had specified a detour in the wake of the protest march from the Congress party’s Queen’s Road office to Raj Bhavan

     DHNS, Bengaluru,  

JUN 17 2022, 00:52 IST | UPDATED: JUN 17 2022, 08:13 IST

The Congress party’s protest threw traffic out of gear in central parts of Bengaluru on Thursday

The traffic police had specified a detour in the wake of the protest march frorn the Congress party’s Queen’s Road office to Raj Bhavan. However, many vehicle usersseemed unezwareandendedup sc sais getting stuck in a bumper-to-bumper traffic jam.

Queen’s Road, Cunningham Road, Infantry Road, Ambedkar Veedhi, Raj Bhavan Road and Cubbon Road were the worst affected.

Traffic crawled at Basaveshwara Circle, Palace Road, Ballari Road, Mysore Bank Circle, Corporation Circle, KG Road and the surrounding streets.

Shivajinagar-bound buses from northern Bengaluru localities such as Hebba were stopped at entrance of Queen’s Road, stranding passengers midway.
